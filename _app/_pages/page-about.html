Chris Coles

I am a creator, first. I love writing code. I am driven by empowering people through creating intuitive technological solutions.

I enjoy the constant challenge that crafting flexible, modular code poses. I believe that simplicity is the ultimate form of sophistication and the solutions that are created should be as intuitive as they are powerful.

Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.

Writing good code is like writing a book.

You begin with a word. Compose a few more words, and you will have a sentence. Write another sentence and, stop.

Rewrite those sentences until perfection has been achieved. Repeat, and soon those sentences turn in to paragraphs, paragraphs into pages and pages into books.

Creation is incremental and purposeful.
